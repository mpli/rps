Pricing Note

  • Prices above are for a typical pool and could be higher for large or more demanding pools.
  • The monthly service charges are averaged out based on year-round service and they include the Holiday and Vacation policy outlined below.
  • Before we make a commitment, we will need to see the pool, test the chemicals and check the equipment. There could be an initial one time extra charge if the pool water balance or equipment condition is not in good enough shape to start regular weekly service.

    • Service Note

  • It is your responsibility to add or drain water to or from your pool as needed. We will leave a note indicating when we see the need for it and you need to take care of it.
  • We will adjust pool pump timers, or ask you to adjust them if we do not have access to the controller, to apropriate daily run times for the season. It is critical for proper filtration and algae prevention.
  • When selecting one of the lower weekly service levels, you should take into consider your dedication to performing any service items not included. Review the "Treatment of yellow algae ..." write up in our Pool Tips web page.

    • Holiday Policy

    If your regularly scheduled service day falls on the following holidays, we will provide chemical-only service on that day, the day before, or the day after - regardless of your service level. We will take care of anything we notice that requires immediate attention.

    • New Year's Eve and New Year's Day
    • Memorial Day
    • Independence Day
    • Labor Day

    We will not be perfoming pool service on the following holidays. We will make sure that pool chemicals will last until the next service day. If you notice any problem contact us and we will schedule time to take care of it.

    • Thanksgiving Day and the day after Thanksgiving
    • Christmas Eve and Christmas Day

    Vacation Policy

    We will take up to 4 separate one-week vacations during the year, but not during the critical summer months. We will make sure your pool is properly balanced and chemicals will last during the time we take off. We will leave a door hanger informing you one week prior to our vacation time. Our prices reflect us physically servicing your pool 48 weeks in a year.
